project-text-1: >-
  Dynamit Kft. is a leading player in the FMCG sector and the domestic
  distributor of global brands such as Paldo, OMG, OTTOGI, and Lotte. Managing a
  portfolio of this scale and diversity requires a continuous, high-level
  marketing presence, from store shelves all the way to digital displays. Our
  collaboration began at a critical decision point: our partner was originally
  looking for an in-house employee to expand and manage marketing processes, but
  the complexity of the tasks and international expectations ultimately made the
  agency model the right choice.


  The challenge lay in “multi-front” communication. We had to not only manage
  domestic campaigns but also maintain direct, marketing-level contact with
  representatives of foreign brands. This required a partner who could
  understand international brand guidelines at a strategic level while being
  able to adapt them to the specificities of the Hungarian market and consumer
  habits. Our task was to take over full operational and strategic management
  and execution as the external marketing department of Dynamit Kft., following
  an extremely comprehensive concept plan.

  this collaboration is a scalable and professional marketing machine capable of
  successfully driving multiple global brands with distinct personalities in the
  domestic market simultaneously.
