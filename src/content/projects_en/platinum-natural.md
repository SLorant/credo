project-text-1: PLATINUM is one of the most dominant players in the European pet
  food market, with a professional credibility built on a unique manufacturing
  technology (FSG) and uncompromising ingredient selection. Although the brand's
  reputation among pet owners is unquestionable, they recognized the rising
  noise level in the digital space. The goal was to prevent PLATINUM from
  getting lost in the crowd of generic pet foods, and to sharply differentiate
  its premium quality from mass-market products. To achieve this, instead of
  merely advertising product benefits, we implemented an omnichannel strategy
  that provided standalone, brand-consistent visual content and a social media
  presence for both dog food and MeatCrisp cat food lines.

project-text-2: >-
  The strategy was rooted in the absolute coherence of digital touchpoints. The
  project launched with the execution of an exciting campaign, for which we
  designed conversion-optimized website banners and cover images supporting an
  exclusive gift bundle promotion, creating a premium feel right from the very
  first click. To increase brand trust and Customer Lifetime Value (CLV), we

  showcases with entertaining content to build a more direct and personal
  connection with the audience.

  The precise targeting and continuously optimized advertising framework applied
  during the campaign delivered outstanding results. Thanks to this strategic
  approach, we not only maximized website traffic but also significantly
  increased Average Order Value (AOV), generating an exceptional, nearly 40x
  Return on Ad Spend (ROAS) for our partner. While strictly adhering to the
  rigorous Brand Book guidelines, this integrated approach simultaneously
  strengthened and expanded the brand's digital ecosystem and loyal customer
  base.
