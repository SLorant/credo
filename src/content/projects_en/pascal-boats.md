  framework, fully supported by consistent Facebook content creation. These

  pre-qualified leads into a fresh, continuously updating system. With this
  approach, we did not just establish the brand’s long-term visibility in the
  Hungarian market; we integrated a scalable, highly cost-efficient business
  development channel into the core operations of Pascal Boats.
